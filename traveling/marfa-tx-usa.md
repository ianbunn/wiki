# Marfa, TX

## Shop & Sight seeing

* [Marfa Book Company](http://www.marfabookco.com/)
  * Eclectic bookstore with cool art posters and random, rare books
* [Wrong Marfa](http://www.wrongmarfa.com/)
  * Pricey art boutique
